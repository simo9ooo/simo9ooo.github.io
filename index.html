üè• OHIP Fertility Coverage in Ontario

Ontario residents may be eligible for one funded cycle of IVF through OHIP. Coverage includes women under 43 years old and requires a referral from a physician. Additional cycles and certain medications may require out-of-pocket payment or private insurance.
